You Belong Here

At Agile Counseling, LGBTQIA+ clients don't have to educate their therapist, defend their identity, or wonder if they'll be accepted. You can focus on what brought you to therapy — not on whether you're safe.

What "Affirming" Means to Us

Affirming care means we don't just tolerate your identity — we celebrate it. We understand that being LGBTQIA+ is not a problem to be solved, but an integral part of who you are. Our role is to support your mental health and wellbeing while honoring your authentic self.

How We Can Help

A supportive space to explore questions about your sexual orientation, gender identity, or expression without judgment or pressure.

Coming Out Support

Navigate the coming out process — deciding if, when, and how to share your identity with family, friends, or coworkers.

Family & Relationship Issues

Work through family rejection, find acceptance, navigate relationships, or help family members understand and support you.

Gender Transition Support

Compassionate support through social, medical, or legal aspects of gender transition at your own pace.

Minority Stress & Discrimination

Anxiety, Depression & Beyond

Address mental health concerns in a context that understands how identity intersects with your experiences and challenges.
