About Candice Gonzalez

Home / About

Candice Gonzalez, LCSW

Licensed Clinical Social Worker

With 22 years of clinical experience, Candice Gonzalez is a trusted therapist in Sandy, Utah dedicated to helping individuals, children, and families navigate life's challenges and find their path to healing. She specializes in anxiety, depression, ADHD, and LGBTQIA+ affirming care, serving clients throughout the Salt Lake Valley including Draper, South Jordan, West Jordan, and Salt Lake City.

Candice takes a collaborative, integrative approach to therapy, drawing from evidence-based methods including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), Art Therapy, Family Systems Therapy, Person-Centered Therapy, and Attachment-Based Therapy. She tailors her approach to meet the unique needs of each client, helping them develop new coping strategies.

Candice is deeply committed to creating a safe, supportive, and nonjudgmental space where clients of all ages and backgrounds feel welcomed and valued. She believes that everyone deserves access to compassionate care and works to ensure that each person who walks through her door feels heard, respected, and empowered to make meaningful changes in their life.

Our Approach

Our Therapeutic Approach

At Agile Counseling, we believe that healing happens within the context of a trusting, collaborative relationship. Candice takes a person-centered approach, meeting each client where they are and honoring their unique story, strengths, and goals. By integrating evidence-based techniques with genuine compassion, we help clients of all ages develop the tools they need to thrive.

"I believe that every person, regardless of age or background, deserves a safe space where they can be fully themselves. My goal is to walk alongside my clients on their journey, helping them discover their own resilience and capacity for growth."
-- Candice Gonzalez, LCSW

Person-Centered Care

Your unique experiences, values, and goals guide every aspect of your treatment. We meet you where you are and move at your pace.

Evidence-Based Methods

We use proven therapeutic approaches including CBT, DBT, Art Therapy, and more to ensure effective, lasting results.

Holistic Approach

We address the whole person -- mind, body, and relationships -- recognizing that true healing involves every aspect of your life.
