Meet Candice Gonzalez, LCSW

Candice Gonzalez is a Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 22 years of experience helping individuals, children, and families navigate life's challenges. Her approach is warm, collaborative, and tailored to each client's unique needs.

At Agile Counseling, we believe that everyone deserves access to compassionate, high-quality mental health care. Using evidence-based approaches including CBT, DBT, Art Therapy, and Family Systems Therapy, Candice creates a safe space where healing and growth can flourish.
